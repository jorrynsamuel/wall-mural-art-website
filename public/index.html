Thihandra Anand

A talented wall mural artist based in Coimbatore, known for creating stunning artworks that transform spaces into vibrant and meaningful environments. With a passion for art since childhood, Thihandra has completed numerous projects, including murals for clinics, salons, garages, and school events. Each project reflects a unique theme and concept, from tranquil green tones promoting inner peace to vibrant floral designs for photo spots. Thihandra's skills include proficiency in various painting techniques, a strong understanding of color theory, and exceptional creativity in bringing artistic visions to life. With a portfolio showcasing diverse talents in mural painting and canvas art, Thihandra is poised for continued success in the field.

Apurva Anand

A visionary artist dedicated to curating mindful experiences through a diverse array of artistic mediums. With a passion for artistry that transcends traditional boundaries, Apurva specializes in painting, illustration, lettering, and engraving, each imbued with profound meaning and intention. Through Apurva's creations, viewers are invited to embark on a journey of self-reflection and discovery, exploring themes of mindfulness, spirituality, and personal growth. From intricately detailed illustrations to thoughtfully crafted engravings, Apurva's work resonates deeply with audiences seeking to connect with themselves and the world around them. As a curator of mindful experiences, Apurva inspires and empowers others through the transformative power of art.

Nina

A dynamic live artist renowned for her captivating live painting performances and exquisite wedding portraits. Based on her passion for art and storytelling, Nina seamlessly translates the emotions and memories of special occasions onto canvas, creating timeless treasures for couples to cherish. Beyond weddings, Nina's artistic talents extend to crafting and selling a diverse range of artistic creations, from custom-made gifts to unique home decor pieces. With a keen eye for detail and a flair for creativity, Nina's artworks captivate audiences and collectors alike, making her a sought-after artist in the realm of live painting and artistic merchandise.
